Cinnamon Rolls

  • 2 c milk
  • 1/2 c Vegetable Oil
  • 1/2 c sugar
  • 1 package yeast
  • 4 c flour
  • 3/4 t baking powder
  • 1/2 t baking soda
  • 1 1/2 t salt
  • 1/2 c flour
  • melted butter, cinnamon, and sugar
  • Heat step 1 till warm, add yeast
  • add flour and stir till just combined. Cover and let rise for 2 hour
  • Add step 4 ingredients, mix thoroughly.
  • Use dough right away or refrigerate up to 3 days.
  • Roll dough into a thin rectangle, spread on melted butter, cinnamon, and sugar. Roll tightly, slice 1" thick, and place into buttered pans.
  • Cover and let rise at least 30 minutes
  • Bake a 375, makes approximately 24 rolls
  • icing: 1/2 bag powdered sugar, 1T maple syrup, 2 T melted butter, 2 T coffe, dash of salt, milk to get icing to the right consistency.
